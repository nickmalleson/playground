{\LARGE Collected Research Papers of Jiaqi Ge\par}
\vspace{2em}

This book brings together a selection of Jiaqi's published papers, reflecting the breadth of her work and her contribution to agent-based modelling. 
The papers were identified via her ORCID record, and some additional papers have been submitted by colleagues.
If the paper was publicly-available, the full text has been included. 
Where this was not possible, we have included bibliographic details. 
Together, they form a small record of Jiaqi’s academic legacy.
